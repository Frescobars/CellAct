Welcome to our Boxing Club! Here at our club, we are more than just a place to train; we are a community dedicated to the art and discipline of boxing. With two thriving locations in Fushe Kosove and Prishtine, we take pride in providing a dynamic and supportive environment for individuals of all skill levels.

Meet Our CEO: Gazmend Gashi

Gazmend Gashi, the driving force behind our club, is not only an experienced and dedicated trainer but also a leader with a passion for the sport. His journey in the world of boxing is marked by a solid educational background, a wealth of experience, and a commitment to fostering a love for martial arts.

Educational Background:

  • Primary School "Meto Bajraktari" - Prishtine (1999-2009)
  • Technical High School "28 Nentori" - Prishtine (2009-2013)
  • College AAB, Faculty of Physical Education and Sport, Prishtine (2013-2021), Bachelor of Physical Education and Sport
  • University of Prishtina "Hasan Prishtina" – Prishtine, Faculty of Physical Education and Sport (2021 – ongoing), Master's Program for Sports Coaching

Gazmend has not only excelled academically but also gained practical experience as a licensed coach by the Boxing Federation of Kosovo. His expertise spans the realms of sports training theory, advanced physiology, biomechanics, and motor skills testing.

Professional Journey:

  • Boxing Club Prishtina – Prishtine (2017 – 2020): Coach

    Gazmend played a vital role in planning and organizing training programs, enhancing technical, tactical, and physical skills for athletes competing at national and international levels.

  • Martial Arts Center Cellactive – Prishtine (2022 - ongoing): Manager

    As the manager of the martial arts center, Gazmend oversees programs for boxing, kickboxing, taekwondo, and jiu-jitsu. His responsibilities include promoting the significance of martial arts for health and self-defense, organizing competitions, and other events.
